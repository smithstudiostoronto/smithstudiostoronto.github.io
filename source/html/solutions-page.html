Our Process

1

Design

Activities during this phase of work include interviews, reviews of existing systems and reports, reviews of existing data, brainstorming, and prioritization.

2

Development

At this point, we translate the selected solution option into detailed plans, and we begin to build. The process is iterative, with frequent review and adjustments as needed by our joint project teams.

2

Testing

Unit, integration and End to End tests are performed. Employee training on the developed system begins.

2

Live

Deployment or “Go-Live,” is when your staff begins to use the system. A final data migration is performed, system documentation is completed, and plans for user support are activated.
