Our Process

Discovery & Design

The foundation of your product. Our Discovery and Design phase is where we wire-frame and storyboard your app, crafting the experience to your users.

Development

This is where the coding happens. Our team plans and delivers in sprints. Each sprint results in an increment which is a potentially shippable product, complete with a product demo.

Quality Assurence

The objective of this step is to clear out all remaining bugs and ensure that the product is working properly on all the right devices. The relevant stakeholders are involved in the final approval of the product.

Distrubution

This includes submission of the app to the relevant app stores, overseen by a Release Manager.
